Hello, my name is Mary Cooke. I am a wife, mother, sister, daughter, aunt, friend, college student, and staff member at The University of Akron. My husband and I have five wonderful children and two dogs, a regal golden retriever, and a crazy yellow lab. Of course, we love each other and both our dogs! I have been at The University of Akron for almost eight years, and I currently work in the Dean's Office of the School of Law. My area is Law Student Affairs, and it is my privilege to assist our students in any way needed.

I have lived in Akron all my life, though I have had the opportunity to visit Italy, India, Iran (before the revolution in 1978), Portugal, France, and to live in a remote village of Honduras. These experiences have given me a deep appreciation of diversity and cultures throughout the world. I never imagined I would live the majority of my life in Akron, but it is a good place to be.
